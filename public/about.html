About Total Sports Live

Zimbabwe’s home for live sports – epic.totalsportslive.co.zw

Who We Are

Total Sports Live is Zimbabwe’s premier digital sports platform, delivering real-time scores, live match streaming, breaking news, and in-depth analysis across football, rugby, cricket, basketball, and local sports.

Our Mission

To connect every Zimbabwean sports fan with the action they love – instantly, reliably, and for free. We bridge the gap between fans and the game, whether you're in Harare, Bulawayo, or beyond.

Why Choose Us

  • Live scores and commentary – updated second by second
  • Exclusive local coverage of Zimbabwe Premier Soccer League, Castle Lager, and schools sports
  • Low-data streaming mode for fans on the go
  • 100% free access – no subscriptions

Our Team

We are a passionate group of Zimbabwean sports journalists, data specialists, and broadcast engineers united by one belief: every fan deserves a front-row seat.
