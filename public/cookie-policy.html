Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 2025

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences and understand how you use our site.

How We Use Cookies

We use the following categories:

  • Essential: Required for site navigation and live score updates. Cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics: Help us count visitors and see which pages are most popular (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional: Remember your language or stream quality preferences.

Third-Party Cookies

We may embed live streams from partners like YouTube or Facebook, which set their own cookies. We do not control these.

Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies via your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect live score functionality.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Continued use of our site constitutes acceptance.
